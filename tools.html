How can you earn through affiliate marketing programs.

Want to know how can you earn by doing affiliate marketing?

Every little detail like, after logging in with your credentials, you went to "Appearence > Themes" and installed your theme. How did you install Yoast SEO in your WordPress setup. How did you created your first blog post. How did you set up focused keyword in Yoast SEO. Every details.

You see you have provided this amount of value in your post your visitors will be benefited from you. They begin to trust you. They will become your lifetime audience.
